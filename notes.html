On this page, you can find notes and handouts for various courses I took, and now teach, as part of the Mathematical Tripos at Cambridge.

Part IB Electromagnetism Handouts

From 2020 to 2022, I supervised the Part IB Electromagnetism course. This course is an introduction to Maxwell's theory of electromagnetism, focussing on electrostatics, magnetostatics, induction, elecomagnetic radiation and some relativistic aspects of electromagnetism. Here is a short revision handout comparing the solution of electrostatic problems with magnetostatic problems.
